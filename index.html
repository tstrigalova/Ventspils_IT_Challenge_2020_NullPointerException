The nature's greatest enemy is a creature named human. Also Latvia hardly has any enviromental issues. On the other hand, we do have demographic issues. Our population is dwindling each year. Our lands are capable of supporting much more people. Therefore we made a pretty accurate visual simulator on Latvia's population. We take data from https://www.csb.gov.lv/lv/statistika/statistikas-temas/iedzivotaji/iedzivotaju-skaits/meklet-tema/387-demografija-2019. Each year data from the previous year is modified so that every single person ages, new babies are born at the average year of reproduction, people die at the life expectancy's age, people imigrate and emigrate at the age of 20-30.
